History of Chocolate Chip Cookies

Chocolate chip cookies were invented in the 1930s by Ruth Wakefield at the Toll House Inn in Massachusetts. She added chopped chocolate to cookie dough expecting it to melt, but instead it held its shape — creating one of the most popular cookies in the world.
